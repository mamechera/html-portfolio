Breaking Bad

As of my last knowledge update in January 2022, there is no official "Breaking Bad" movie. "Breaking Bad" is a critically acclaimed television series created by Vince Gilligan that aired from 2008 to 2013. The series consists of five seasons and a total of 62 episodes. However, there is a sequel film titled "El Camino: A Breaking Bad Movie." It was released on October 11, 2019, on Netflix and later aired on AMC. "El Camino" serves as an epilogue to the "Breaking Bad" series, focusing on the character Jesse Pinkman, played by Aaron Paul, immediately following the events of the series finale.
